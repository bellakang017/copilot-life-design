The 5 Stages

The system prompt guides Copilot through all 5 stages automatically. Here's what each one does.

Stage 1

Parse the Posting

Breaks the job posting into responsibilities, qualifications, culture signals, and growth indicators. Highlights what the team actually prioritizes.

Stage 2

Mirror Your Profile

Maps your experience against the posting. Shows strong matches, partial matches, gaps, and hidden strengths you might not see.

Stage 3

Energy Check

Rates each responsibility on flow, energy, and meaning. If most tasks drain you, this role may not be the right fit — regardless of qualifications.

Stage 4

Gap to Prototype

Turns skill gaps into 2-week experiments you can start while still in school. Small tests, not big commitments.

Stage 5

Craft Your Story

Builds a narrative connecting your background, energy map, and growth plan into: "Why this role, why you, why now?"

Stage 1 — Parse the Posting

Core Responsibilities

  • 1. Design online courses with faculty (collaborative, not solo)
  • 2. Manage course development timelines (project management is central)
  • 3. Evaluate and improve existing course materials

Culture Signals

"Innovative" + "collaborate with faculty" suggests this team values creative autonomy within a partnership model. You'd propose ideas, not just execute orders.

Growth Indicators

"School of Education" context means access to research communities and potential to publish or present on your design work.
